Staff benefit

e4 Healthcare Services is committed to going above and beyond to ensure that every shift is staffed with experienced and skilled professionals. We prioritize fostering genuine business relationships with our clients, supporting them in achieving their health goals and expectations. Through open and clear communication, we ensure our clients are fully involved in every aspect of their health and well-being. We offer transparent payment options, allowing staff to choose between weekly and fortnightly payments. Our commitment to our staff is reflected in:

  1. Generous wages.
  2. Staff appreciation and rewards.
  3. Incentives if you refer your experienced colleagues.
  4. Staff acknowledgement and rewards.

Our dedication to both client care and staff satisfaction sets us apart as a dependable healthcare service provider.

Join Our Team

e4 Healthcare is not just a healthcare service; it’s a community-led by nurses who are deeply committed to providing exceptional care to clients and their families. Our strength lies in our team’s dedication and the high-quality care we deliver. We’re looking for individuals who:

  1. Provides care with love and respect, prioritizing people’s health and well-being.
  2. Understands the importance of quality and safety outcomes for healthcare providers.
  3. Possesses the necessary checks for working with children and NDIS Clearance.
  4. Shows flexibility and adaptability.
  5. Has up-to-date immunizations and COVID-19 vaccinations.
  6. Have years of experience and hold NDIS clearance.
  7. Excel in early morning shifts, with the flexibility to cover all hours on an ad hoc basis.
  8. Have years of experience and hold NDIS clearance.

Meet our requirements for experienced staff:

  1. 24 months of relevant industry experience.
  2. National police clearance under 6 months old.
  3. Qualifications: Certificate III Nurse Assistant or AHPRA registration.
  4. Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
